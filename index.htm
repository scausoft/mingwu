I am currently looking for highly motivated graduated and undergraduate students to work in Security and Privacy in Cloud Computing and Big Data Processing. Students with strong hands-on skills and/or mathematical background are welcomed.

 

Research area is Information Security and Privacy Preservation:

  • Protocols and Systems Resilient to Secret Information Leakage (SIR/LR)
  • Public-Key Cryptosystem and Applications (PKC)
  • Secure Multiparty Computation and Outsourcing Computation (SMC/OC)
  • Security, Obfuscation and Privacy in Big-data (SOP)

Dr. Mingwu Zhang is now a professor at the School of Computer Sciences in Hubei University of Technology. Prior to joining HBUT, he was a JSPS Postdoctoral Fellow at Information Security Takagi's Lab, Faculty of Mathematics in Kyushu University from Aug 2010 to Mar 2011, and Institute of Mathematics for Industry in Kyushu University in Japan from Apr 2011 to Aug 2012 with professor Tsuyoshi Takagi, respectively. Also, he was an Associate Professor at College of Informatic in South China Agricultural University from 2006 to 2010 and an Assistant Professor (lecturer) from 2003 to 2005, and an Assitant Professor at Hubei Polytechnic University from 2000 to 2002, respectively. From Jun 2015 to Jun 2016, he was a Visiting Senior Fellow at School of Computing and Information Technology, University of Wollongong, Australia. He is a senior member of CACR, CCF, IEEE Computer Society and ACM.
